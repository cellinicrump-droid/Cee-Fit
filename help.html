Train smart · User guide
Open Cee Fit

1Put it on your home screen

Android: open the link in Chrome, tap the ⋮ menu, then Add to Home screen.

iPhone: open the link in Safari, tap the Share button, then Add to Home Screen.

Now it opens like a regular app — no app store needed.

2Create your split

Go to Splits and tap ＋ New split.

Name it, pick how many workout days you want (1–7), then name each day — like Push, Pull, or Leg Day. You can give each day a color and pin it to a weekday if you like.

Tap Create split and it's ready.

3Add your exercises

Open a day and tap Add section to make groups like Warm-up, Chest, or Cardio.

Then tap Add exercises. Browse by muscle group or use Search 870+ exercises and tap the ones you want.

Don't see it? Tap Custom exercise and type your own — kickboxing class, dog walk, anything.

4Log your workout

The Today screen shows your day. Tap the tabs at the top to switch days.

Use the − and ＋ buttons to set your sets, reps, and weight, and check off each exercise as you finish it. There's a notes box under every exercise for anything you want to remember.

Your numbers save automatically as you go.

5Back up your data

Everything is stored on your phone only — private to you.

On the Splits screen, tap Backup data to save a file, and Restore to load it back — like on a new phone.

A quick backup once in a while keeps your progress safe.

?Quick answers

Is it free? Yes. No account, no ads, no catch.

Is my data private? Yes — workouts live on your phone, nowhere else.

Do I need internet? Only for the exercise pictures. Logging works offline.
